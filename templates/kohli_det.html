Virat Kohli - The Cricket Legend

About Virat Kohli

Full Name: Virat Kohli

Birthdate: November 5, 1988

Birthplace: Delhi, India

Profession: Professional Cricketer

Role: Top-order batsman

Batting Style: Right-handed

Career Achievements

- Former captain of the Indian national cricket team.

- Over 70 international centuries across all formats.

- Fastest player to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000 runs in ODIs.

- ICC Cricketer of the Year in 2017 and 2018.

- Key player in India's 2011 Cricket World Cup victory.

Personal Life

Virat Kohli is married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma. The couple got married in December 2017 and have a daughter named Vamika.

He is also known for his fitness regime and is considered a fitness icon in India.
